# KB005 — Hardware (laptop / monitor / printer / peripherals)

**Applies to:** Company laptops, monitors, docking stations, printers, peripherals.

## Symptoms
- Printer shows a jam/error; nobody on a floor can print.
- Laptop won't power on, no display, or a dock/peripheral not detected.

## L1 resolution steps
1. For printers: open and reseat all trays, check for a real jam, then power-cycle the printer.
2. Confirm the printer is online and the queue isn't paused; clear stuck jobs.
3. For displays: reseat video cables, try a different cable/port, and test the dock with another laptop.
4. For "won't power on": confirm charger/LED, hold power 10s, try a known-good charger.
5. Capture the asset tag / serial for any device that needs a part or swap.

## When to escalate
- Suspected hardware failure (no power, dead screen) → log a repair/replacement with asset details.
- Shared/network printer down for a whole floor → escalate to Print/Network support.
