# KB002 — VPN & WiFi connectivity

**Applies to:** Corporate VPN client, office and home WiFi.

## Symptoms
- VPN won't connect, or drops every few minutes.
- WiFi connected but "no internet"; cannot reach internal sites.

## L1 resolution steps
1. Confirm the user has internet access without VPN (open a public site).
2. Have the user fully quit and relaunch the VPN client, then reconnect.
3. Check for a recent client/OS update; if VPN broke right after an update, note the version.
4. Toggle WiFi off/on and forget/rejoin the network; for home use, suggest rebooting the router.
5. For "no internet" on office WiFi, confirm the SSID is the corporate one, not guest.

## When to escalate
- Multiple users in one office affected at once → likely a site outage, escalate to Network team.
- VPN authentication fails for everyone after a change → escalate to Network/Security.
